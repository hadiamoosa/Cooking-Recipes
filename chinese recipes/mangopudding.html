Mango Pudding

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.4 (210 reviews)

Ingredients

  • Mango cubes 400g
  • Water as required
  • Sagudana (Tapioca sago) ½ Cup
  • Nariyal (Fresh coconut) 50g
  • Pakola Full cream milk 1 litre
  • Sugar ½ Cup or to taste
  • Elaichi powder (Cardamom powder) ½ tsp
  • Pista (Pistachios) chopped ¼ Cup
  • Badam (Almonds) chopped ¼ Cup
  • Aam (Mango) cubes 400g

Directions

  1. In blender jug,add mango and blend to puree mangoes & set aside.
  2. In kettle,add water and bring it to boil.
  3. Add tapioca sago,mix well & cook on medium flame for 12-14 minutes or until transparent then strain,rinse with water & set aside.
  4. Grate fresh coconut with the help of grater & set aside.
  5. In a wok,add milk,sugar,cardamom powder and mix well,bring it to boil and cook on low flame for 4-5 minutes.
  6. Add mango puree and whisk well.
  7. Add grated coconut,pistachios,almonds,mix well and cook on low flame for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Add cooked tapioca sago,mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Let it cool.
  10. Add mango and give it a good mix.
  11. Garnish with mango,pistachio & fresh coconut.
  12. Serve chilled!

Nutrition Facts
