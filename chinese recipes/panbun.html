Pan Fried Veggies Bun

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.3 (170 reviews)

Ingredients

    Prepare Filling:
  • Cooking oil 3 tbs
  • Gajar (Carrot) julienne ½ Cup
  • Band gobhi (Cabbage) shredded 1 Cup
  • Corn kernels boiled ½ Cup
  • Shimla mirch (Capsicum) julienne ½ Cup
  • Zucchini grated & squeezed ½ Cup
  • Mushrooms sliced ¼ Cup
  • Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger garlic paste) 1 tsp
  • Himalayan Pink salt 1 tsp or to taste
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
  • Paprika powder 1 tsp
  • Chicken powder ½ tbs (optional)
  • Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed 1 tsp
  • Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  • Soy sauce 1 & ½ tbs
  • Hara pyaz (Spring onion) chopped handful
  • Water as required
  • Til (Sesame seeds) white & black as required
  • Cooking oil 3-4 tbs
  • Water ¼ Cup
    • Prepare Dough:
  • Maida (All-purpose flour) sifted 3 Cups
  • Baking soda ½ tsp
  • Himalayan Pink Salt 1 tsp or to taste
  • Lukewarm water ¾ Cup or as required
  • Cooking oil ½ tsp
    • Prepare Sauce:
  • Soy sauce 4 tbs
  • Sirka (Vinegar) 2 tbs
  • Lal micrh (Red chilli) crushed 1 tsp
  • Himalayan Pink Salt ¼ tsp
  • Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 1 tsp
  • Sugar powdered 1 tsp
  • Til (Sesame seeds) 1 tsp
  • Hara pyaz (Spring onion) chopped 1 tbs
  • Til ka tel (Sesame oil) 1 tsp

Directions

    Prepare Filling:
  1. In a wok add cooking oil, julienne carrot, shredded cabbage, corn kernels, julienne capsicum, zucchini, mushrooms & sauté for 2 minutes on medium flame.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste, pink salt, black pepper powder, paprika powder, chicken powder, red chilli crushed, white pepper powder, soy sauce, mix well & cook for 2-3 minutes on high flame.
  3. Add spring onion, mix well then set aside & let it cool.
    4. Prepare Dough:
  4. In a bowl add all-purpose flour, baking soda, pink salt & mix well.
  5. Gradually add water, mix well & knead until dough is formed,cover & let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
    6. Prepare Veggie Buns:
  6. Place the dough on a flat surface, sprinkle flour & knead the dough again.
  7. Take a small dough (50 g) & make a smooth ball, sprinkle flour than roll out with rolling pin.
  8. Add 2 tbs of prepared filling in the center of rolled dough, brush water on the edge of dough than gather the dough from all sides, twist & make a bun.
  9. Dip the flat surface of bun in water & coat the surface with sesame seeds then set aside.
  10. In a frying pan add cooking oil, prepared veggie buns (sesame seeds side down) & fry on low flame until golden.
  11. Flip the buns & fry again from other side until golden. Add water in frying pan, then cover & cook on low flame until water is completely dried (5-6 minutes) (makes 12).
    12. Prepare Sauce:
  12. In a bowl add soy sauce, vinegar, red chilli crushed, pink salt, chopped garlic, sugar powder, sesame seeds, spring onion, sesame oil & mix well.
  13. Serve Pan fried veggie buns with prepared sauce!
