Chicken Fried Rice

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.4 (220 reviews)

Ingredients

  • Chawal (Rice) 2 Cups (soaked & boiled)
  • Oil 2 tbs
  • Anday (Egg) beaten 2
  • Gajar (Carrot) finely chopped ¾ Cup
  • Bund gobhi (Cabbage) finely chopped ¾ Cup
  • Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  • Chicken powder 1 tsp
  • Cheeni (Sugar) 1 tsp
  • Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
  • Hara pyaz (Green onion) leaves chopped ¾ Cup
  • Til ka tel (Sesame oil) 1 tsp

Directions

  1. On tray,spread boiled rice,and let them cool & set aside.
  2. In wok,add oil,eggs and mix well.
  3. Add carrots,cabbage and mix well for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add boiled rice,mix & toss well.
  5. Add white pepper powder,chicken powder,sugar,salt,green onion and give it a good mix.
  6. Add sesame oil,mix well & serve.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 350 | Protein: 20g | Carbs: 45g | Fat: 10g
