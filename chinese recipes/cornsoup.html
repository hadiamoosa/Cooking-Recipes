Chicken Corn Soup

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.6 (190 reviews)

Ingredients

  • Chicken & vegetable stock 6 cups
  • Chicken boiled & shredded 1 & ½ cup
  • Cream of corn or sweet corn boiled 1 & ½ cup
  • Salt 1 tsp or to taste
  • Water 1 cup + corn flour 4-5 tbs
  • Egg whites 2
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper powder to taste
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • Vinegar to taste

Directions

  1. In a pot add chicken stock and bring it to boil.
  2. Add chicken and corn and cook for 8-10 mins.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Mix corn flour in water and add gradually. Cook until desired thickness.
  5. Beat 2 egg whites and add while stirring continuously.
  6. Chicken corn soup is ready.
  7. Serve with soy sauce, vinegar, salt & pepper, chili sauce as per taste.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 150 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 18g | Fat: 4g
