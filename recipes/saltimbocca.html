Sandy's Chicken Saltimbocca

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8 (320 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 8 fresh sage leaves
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Fresh sage leaves for garnish

Directions

  1. Pound chicken breasts to an even thickness. Place 2 slices of prosciutto and a sage leaf on each chicken breast.
  2. Roll up each chicken breast and secure with toothpicks.
  3. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Dredge chicken in the mixture, shaking off excess.
  4. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add white wine and chicken broth to the skillet, and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Remove chicken and set aside. Stir lemon juice into the sauce, and simmer until slightly thickened.
  7. Return chicken to the skillet to warm through. Garnish with fresh sage leaves before serving.
