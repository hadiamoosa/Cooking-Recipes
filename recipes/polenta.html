Polenta

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.5 (200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups yellow cornmeal
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil in a heavy large saucepan. Add salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in cornmeal. Reduce heat to low and cook until mixture thickens and cornmeal is tender, stirring often, about 15 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat. Add butter and stir until melted.
  4. Transfer the polenta to a bowl and serve.

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 160 | Protein: 3g | Carbs: 31g | Fat: 3g
