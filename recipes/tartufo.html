Tartufo

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.8 (150 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1/2 cup chocolate ice cream, softened
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup maraschino cherries
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup chocolate syrup
  • 1/2 cup crushed biscotti

Directions

  1. Line a small bowl with plastic wrap and press softened vanilla ice cream into the bowl, creating a well in the center.
  2. Spoon chocolate ice cream into the well and insert a maraschino cherry into the center. Top with additional vanilla ice cream to cover.
  3. Wrap the ice cream ball in plastic wrap and freeze until firm.
  4. Roll the frozen ice cream ball in cocoa powder and crushed biscotti to coat evenly.
  5. Drizzle with chocolate syrup and serve immediately.
