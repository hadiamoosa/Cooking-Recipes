Spicy Tuna Puttanesca

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.4 (150 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons capers, drained
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2 cans (5 ounces each) tuna in olive oil, drained
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, sauté until fragrant.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, olives, capers, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Add tuna to the sauce, breaking it into chunks. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce until well coated.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
