Chicken Marsala

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.5 (200 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth

Directions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Coat chicken in the mixture.
  2. Melt butter with oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken on both sides. Add mushrooms, wine, and chicken broth.
  3. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, turning once, until chicken is cooked through.
